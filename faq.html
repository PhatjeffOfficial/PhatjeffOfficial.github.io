Frequently asked questions

What do we do?

We are a not-for-profit mental health organization that is commited to helping everyone recover from the COVID-19 pandemic, as well as boost Calgary's financial prosperity. Read more about us here.

How do we help?

We are a free, not-for-profit mental health organization based in Calgary, Alberta. We provide non-judgemental counseling and live chat 24/7.

What made you do this?

I, myself have strugged with mental illnesses and knows what it feels like to be going through this. So, I wanted everybody to know that they are not alone and they can always have somebody trustworthy to confide in. This and my passion for coding made me develop this website.

I am in crisis. Where do I get help?

Although we try our best to help others, we do not replace crisis hotlines. Check our blog for important mental health hotlines.
